Private Tavern

Whether you're looking to unwind with chardonnay by the indoor fireplace or celebrate with IPAs until 3 in the morning, we got you covered. Northern Lodge houses our own private tavern with free alcohol. The Private Tavern is open from 11am to 3am, 7 days a week. (included in your stay).

Hiking and Biking

Maine claims award winning trails for hiking and mountain biking so it is important that we provide the most enjoyable and safest trails the state has to offer. Northern Lodge employs a full staff of professionally trained security and safety personel that are on duty 24/7 for your convinience.

Dining Room

Breakfast can only be enjoyed when served on a personal level, that's why at Northern Lodge, we have a fully staffed kitchen eager to meet the needs and wants of our guests. Order what you want, as much you want. Our dining room is open from 6am to 12pm, monday-saturday. (included in your stay).

About our Events

Northern Lodge hosts many events ranging from live performances to family festivals. Although these events are open to the public, only Northern Lodge guests are granted free access with VIP status at any Northern Lodge hosted event.
